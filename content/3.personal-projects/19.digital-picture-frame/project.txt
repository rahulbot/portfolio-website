title: Digital Picture Frame

date: Jan 2008

content:
<p>
In the hopes of being awarded yet another geek merit badge, I turned my old PowerBook G3 laptop into a digital picture frame to hang on the wall.  The hinge as broken and the screen about to fall off, so it seemed like a natural conversion to make.
</p>
<p>I started by flipping the screen around and mounting it to the back of the computer.  The more laborious work was creating a wood frame with the jigsaw and trying to put enough holes in it for the computer to vent out it's heat.</p>
