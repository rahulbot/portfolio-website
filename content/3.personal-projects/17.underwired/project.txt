<p>
UnderWired was our submission to the 2005 <a href="http://www.showusyourbra.org/">Show Us Your Bra auction/contest</a>. The idea is simple - you cup your hand over one of the cups of the bra, and it lights up!  It is built with a bra, a capacitive sensing chip, and some electro-luminescent wire.
</p>
